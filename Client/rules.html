Coordinates

Coordinates are in the top right of each tile in the format (x,y)
NOTE: Road and village spots also count towards the system, however, they are too small to display the coordinates on!
It is just simple math
Cites are placed on villages, Villages are placed on intersections of two roads, and roads are placed in between tiles. NOTHING CAN BE PLACED ON RIVERS OR TILES!

Pillager

The pillager when placed on a tile prevents that tile from creating resources. A pillager can be moved when a 7 is rolled or when a Pillage Card is played.
If you have more than 7 resources when a 7 is rolled half of them will be pillaged rounding up (piles with the most resources will be taken from first)
THE PILLAGER MUST BE PLACED ON A TILE!

Trading

Bank Trading

Bank trading is 4 to 1. First enter what you want, then what you will give

Player Trading

First enter how much of resource you will give then how much you will take.
Any players meeting the requirements will be prompted to accept or decline the trade

Victory Points

Victory points are how a winner is decided. Victory Points are given out for many things like building or purchasing cards. 10 Victory Points are required to win the game.
